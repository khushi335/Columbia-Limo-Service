BWI Car Service FAQs

Find answers to common questions about our premium BWI airport transfers.

How does your BWI car service compare to taxis or ride-sharing apps?

Our service provides a level of reliability and luxury that apps cannot match:

  • Professional chauffeurs: Background-checked and BWI-specialized.
  • Fixed rates: Zero surge pricing; you know the total upfront.
  • Flight tracking: We monitor your arrival in real-time.
  • Meet & greet: Your driver waits at baggage claim with a sign.
How far in advance should I book BWI airport transportation?

We recommend booking 24-48 hours in advance for guaranteed vehicle selection. However, we often accommodate urgent requests with 2-3 hours notice based on current fleet availability.

What happens if my flight is delayed or early?

We track every flight. If you're late, we wait; if you're early, we're there. We include 60 minutes of complimentary wait time for all domestic arrivals.

Do you provide child car seats?

Yes. We provide safety-inspected rear-facing, forward-facing, and booster seats at no additional charge. Simply note the child's age and weight during your booking.

What is your cancellation policy?

Cancellations made 24+ hours in advance receive a full refund. Cancellations within 4-24 hours incur a 50% fee. Less than 4 hours notice is considered a full charge.

Can you accommodate large groups?

Absolutely. We specialize in high-capacity transport with 6-pax SUVs, 10-pax Vans, and 14-pax Executive Sprinters.

Do you offer corporate accounts?

Yes. Corporate partners benefit from 10-15% discounts, consolidated monthly billing, and priority booking status for executive assistants.
